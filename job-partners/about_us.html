About Us

We’re a group of creative thinkers who have built a business to change the world.

Ideate is a revolutionary platform looking to transform access to opportunities for students and startups around the country. Our mission is to improve student outcomes by providing high quality resources in the form of premium job opportunities, a collaboration space for students to build big projects, and direct outlets to venture funding for ideate projects that turn into startup companies.

For far too long, higher education and entrepreneurship have been spaces that have given unequal access to women and people of color. We believe it’s important to democratize access to these resources in a way that ensures that the “pipeline problem” is no longer an excuse for the lack of diversity in our country’s fastest growing industries.

We firmly believe in the power of curiosity and collaboration. With the tools and network that ideate provides, students are seconds away from a community of passionate individuals looking to help them bring out the best in themselves.

Our Principles

Community

The ideate community is supportive, curious, passionate, and collaborative. There’s no better space to ideate in while your dream is in the ideate stage or beyond.

Funding Opportunities

Financial resources are key to the success of any idea. We want to ensure that anyone with a great idea isn’t hindered by a lack of capital to start.

Job Resources

In addition to our curated education materials, we host job listings to provide additional opportunities on top of anything entrepreneurial related.

Entrepreneurship

Who knows where the vision for world changing ideas lies? We believe the future is in our youth. Through empowering student entrepreneurs we’re fueling the next generation of business leaders.

Diversity

We want to bring students together from all types of background to contribute with their experiences and intellect.

Collaboration

Networks are powerful. We enhance existing social networks by empowering people to connect with highly talented individuals inside and outside of their immediate school networks.
